Coursaice offers course material from SAICE teachers.

Course material from your teachers is now online.

The aim of Coursaice is provide course material from S.A.I.C.E. teachers to anyone who wishes to follow a course all by themselves. It can also be used by teachers to store their notes, so that students can refer to them at any time. If Coursaice is found to be helpful and actively fulfilling its purpose, then more functionalities and features will be added. For now, it's just an experiment to begin with :)

Happy studying!
